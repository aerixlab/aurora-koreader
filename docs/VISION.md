# Aurora Vision

> *What if KOReader felt as beautiful as the books it lets you read?*

---

## Why Aurora Exists

KOReader is one of the most capable reading engines available for e-ink devices.

It is fast, powerful, open source, and supports an incredible range of formats and devices.

But over many years, its interface has grown organically. While feature-rich, the experience can sometimes feel inconsistent, technical, and intimidating—especially for readers who simply want to enjoy their books.

Aurora exists to change that.

Not by replacing KOReader.

But by building a modern, thoughtful experience around it.

---

## Our Philosophy

Aurora is designed around one simple idea:

> Reading should feel calm.

Every screen, every button, every interaction should help readers disappear into their books—not into menus.

We believe software should quietly get out of the way.

---

## What Aurora Is

Aurora is a modern interface framework built on top of KOReader.

It reimagines the reading experience while preserving everything that makes KOReader exceptional.

Aurora focuses on:

- Beautiful design
- Thoughtful navigation
- Reading-first workflows
- Consistent user experience
- Responsive layouts
- Themeable atmospheres
- Performance on e-ink devices

---

## What Aurora Is Not

Aurora is **not** another reading engine.

Aurora does **not** replace KOReader.

Aurora does **not** sacrifice speed for visual effects.

Aurora does **not** chase trends.

Every design decision must improve the reading experience.

---

## Design Principles

### Reading First

Books always come before interface.

---

### Calm

Minimal.
Clean.
Peaceful.

No unnecessary clutter.

---

### Consistency

Every screen should feel like it belongs to the same application.

Spacing.

Typography.

Icons.

Colors.

Interactions.

Everything should follow a single design language.

---

### Simplicity

Powerful software doesn't have to feel complicated.

Advanced features should remain available without overwhelming everyday readers.

---

### Responsive by Default

Aurora is designed to adapt gracefully across different e-ink devices.

From compact 6-inch readers to larger screens, the experience should always feel natural.

---

### Fast Forever

Animations are optional.

Performance is mandatory.

Every feature should respect the limitations and strengths of e-ink technology.

---

## Atmospheres

Aurora doesn't use "themes."

Aurora uses **Atmospheres**.

Each atmosphere represents a different reading mood.

Examples include:

- Frost
- Midnight
- Forest
- Dawn
- Bloom

Each atmosphere combines colors, wallpapers, typography, and visual identity into one cohesive experience.

---

## Our Goal

When someone opens Aurora, we want them to think:

> "This feels like it was designed for reading."

Not for showing features.

Not for showing technology.

Simply for enjoying books.

---

## Long-Term Vision

One day, we hope Aurora becomes the interface people recommend when someone says:

> "I love KOReader, but I wish it looked modern."

If Aurora can make reading a little calmer, a little simpler, and a little more beautiful...

Then it has achieved its purpose.
