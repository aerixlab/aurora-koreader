## Major Design Decisions
Aurora is not trying to be prettier.
It is trying to make reading feel peaceful.

Home Screen Purpose
The Home screen is not a dashboard.

It is the entrance to your personal reading space.

It should gently transition readers into a reading mood.

Reader Companion

We discovered what might become Aurora's signature feature.

Not AI.

## A Reader Companion powered by simple rules.

Examples:

Aurora Picks
Rediscover forgotten books
Continue unfinished books
Gentle reading encouragement

Everything works offline.

Aurora's Voice

Not a chatbot.

Not a productivity coach.

More like:

A thoughtful bookstore owner who quietly recommends a book.

## Navigation

We questioned mobile app conventions.

Instead of large bottom navigation, we're exploring navigation designed specifically for e-readers.

No decision yet—that will come after sketching.

## Design Direction

Aurora should feel:

Calm
Cozy
Quiet
Comfortable
Inviting
Thoughtful

Aurora should never feel:

Busy
Corporate
Technical
Gamified
Overwhelming
